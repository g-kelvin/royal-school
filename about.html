Watch Our Story

Mikindani Royal Comprehensive School Primary is a private primary school in Mikindani. This school is run by private individual. It is a day school. This educational institution is a ordinary and mixed type of institution.The pupil to classroom ratio in this school is 48:1 and the pupil to toilet ratio is 48:1. There are total 4 classrooms, 2 boys toilets, 2 girls toilets and 1 teachers toilet. The total numbers of students enrolls in this school are 192. There are 1 non-teaching staff males and 2 female non-teaching staff in Mikindani Royal Comprehensive School Primary, Mikindani.

Mikindani Royal Comprehensive School Primary is located in Coast province, Mombasa district, Changamwe divison, Mikindani location and Changamwe Constituency. The geographical location map coordinates for this school is -4.00525 (Latitude), 39.62971 (Longitude).

The school provides transport to those pupils coming from the other sides of Mikindani. This service has attracted many parents and many have joined the school and are effectively using the transport.

All those who would like to enquire about the service can come to the school office for enquiries or for list of charges.
